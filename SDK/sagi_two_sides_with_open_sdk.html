SAGI

Search for Artificial General Intelligence
Inspired by SETI · democratising the next model

Borrow the world's idle compute to search for the next leap in AI — open for anyone to build on, and owned by the people who power it.

Today's models are brilliant but can't improve themselves. The compute to find what comes next sits with a handful of companies. SAGI spreads that search across millions of everyday devices.

Two sides of one coin
Side A — the foundation

Deep-tech · the protocol

A SETI- and Bitcoin-style network that borrows idle compute and runs a planetary-scale search. It owns the leaderboards, the token ledger and the wallets.

How the search works

Nodes run bets — candidate model designs

Each is tested locally, scored on efficiency & power

Results ranked on public leaderboards

Contributors earn tokens → redeemable for model usage, or co-ownership if SAGI cracks the next level.

Side B — the scale

Commercial apps · growth

The job: turn the search into things millions of people want to use. Apps that sit on the protocol and feed it.

Passive — runs in the background. Engineers who love the vision. Small but devoted.

Active — people play, and their choices steer the search. Everyone with a phone. Massive.

The active layer supplies the signals you can't automate — preference judgments, scouting good designs, red-teaming. Think Pokémon GO, but the play trains AI.

Ship the flagship game first → then open the SDK so others build the rest.

The growth engine

An open SDK — so anyone can build on SAGI

Side B doesn't stay one game. The SDK opens the application layer so any developer can ship games and tools on top — each one a new front door to the same search.
